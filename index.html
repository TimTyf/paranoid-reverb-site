About the Band

Paranoid Reverb is a heavy rock band from Thessaloniki, Greece, known for their powerful sound that blends crushing riffs, groovy rhythms, and an intense, atmospheric vibe.

In December 2020, they released their debut album Billion Drops, featuring standout tracks like “Maze” and “Rain.” The album introduced the band’s raw energy and emotional depth, gaining attention within the heavy rock and stoner scenes.

Over the years, Paranoid Reverb have shared the stage with iconic acts such as Unida, Planet of Zeus, Dopethrone, Nightstalker, and more—earning a reputation for explosive live performances.

With a new frontman now leading the charge, the band is currently working on their highly anticipated second full-length album, set to mark the next chapter of their sonic evolution.
